How to add sustainability

It is important, when talking about sustainability, to understand that no company and no product is ever 100% sustainable. Everytime we create a product, we use ressources and however sustainable the ressources are, it is still a use of ressources and that is not sustainable. The only real sustainable thing is to reuse products that have already been used, however that’s not really an option for candy or at least not one that we are going to explore.
